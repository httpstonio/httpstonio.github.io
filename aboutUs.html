-About Company-

Tone’s Detailing is a business I started in 2022. I used to detail my truck and post it on my socials which led to my friends asking for me to detail theirs. I then started doing regular washes on the side which led me to investing into more products. As time progressed I realized when I detail I don’t feel like I’m working and that it is something I truly enjoy. That’s when I came up with the idea to start my own business.

Donation Information
